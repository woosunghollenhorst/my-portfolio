Overview

This group project was conducted in partnership with Swapfiets, the world’s largest bicycle subscription service. Our team developed a marketing strategy to increase Swapfiets’ customer base in Karlsruhe, Germany, with a focus on the winter months. We applied the Sinus-Milieus model for market segmentation, conducted primary survey research, and proposed guerrilla marketing campaigns targeting the Escapist milieu.

Note

View the full report: Swapfiets Report (PDF)

Market Segmentation: Sinus-Milieus

Code 
import plotly.graph_objects as go

milieus = ['High Achiever', 'Socio-ecological', 'Escapist', 
           'Movers & Shakers', 'New Middle Class']
share = [27, 16, 15, 10, 8]
colors = ['#5bcefa', '#7c73ff', '#2ecc71', '#e74c3c', '#f39c12']

fig = go.Figure(data=[go.Pie(
    labels=milieus, 
    values=share, 
    marker=dict(colors=colors),
    textinfo='label+percent',
    textfont=dict(color='white', size=12),
    hovertemplate='%{label}: %{value}%<extra></extra>'
)])
fig.update_layout(
    title='Sinus-Milieus Distribution in Karlsruhe',
    template='plotly_dark',
    paper_bgcolor='rgba(0,0,0,0)',
    plot_bgcolor='rgba(0,0,0,0)',
    font=dict(color='#e0e0e0'),
    showlegend=False
)
fig.show()

Survey: Interest by Milieu

Code 
import plotly.graph_objects as go

milieus = ['Movers & Shakers', 'High Achiever', 'Escapist', 'New Middle Class']
interest = [69.23, 64.29, 62.75, 55.00]
colors = ['#2ecc71', '#5bcefa', '#7c73ff', '#f39c12']

fig = go.Figure(data=[go.Bar(
    x=milieus, 
    y=interest, 
    marker_color=colors,
    text=[f'{v}%' for v in interest],
    textposition='outside',
    textfont=dict(color='#e0e0e0'),
    hovertemplate='%{x}: %{y:.2f}%<extra></extra>'
)])
fig.update_layout(
    title='Interest in Swapfiets Subscription by Milieu',
    yaxis_title='Interest (%)',
    yaxis_range=[0, 100],
    template='plotly_dark',
    paper_bgcolor='rgba(0,0,0,0)',
    plot_bgcolor='rgba(0,0,0,0)',
    font=dict(color='#e0e0e0')
)
fig.show()

Potential Market Population

Code 
import plotly.graph_objects as go

groups = ['High Achiever', 'Escapist', 'Movers & Shakers',

Winter Cycling Preferences

Code 
import plotly.graph_objects as go

gadgets = ['Winter Tires', 'Earmuffs', 'Reflective Vest', 'Face Mask', 
           'Gloves', 'Rain Jacket', 'Water Bottle', 'Other']
escapist = [14, 17, 15, 4, 1, 8, 7, 2]
new_middle = [5, 3, 5, 8, 12, 5, 3, 3]
high_achiever = [13, 8, 8, 8, 6, 7, 8, 0]
movers = [1, 3, 5, 11, 5, 2, 1, 1]

fig = go.Figure()
fig.add_trace(go.Bar(name='Escapist', x=gadgets, y=escapist, marker_color='#5bcefa'))
fig.add_trace(go.Bar(name='New Middle Class', x=gadgets, y=new_middle, marker_color='#7c73ff'))
fig.add_trace(go.Bar(name='High Achiever', x=gadgets, y=high_achiever, marker_color='#2ecc71'))
fig.add_trace(go.Bar(name='Movers & Shakers', x=gadgets, y=movers, marker_color='#e74c3c'))

fig.update_layout(
    barmode='group',
    title='Winter Cycling Gadget Preferences by Milieu',
    yaxis_title='Number of Respondents',
    template='plotly_dark',
    paper_bgcolor='rgba(0,0,0,0)',
    plot_bgcolor='rgba(0,0,0,0)',
    font=dict(color='#e0e0e0'),
    xaxis_tickangle=-30,
    legend=dict(x=0.65, y=0.95)
)
fig.show()

Key Findings

  • Escapist milieu was selected as the primary target group due to the greatest market potential (~29,300 people) and strongest alignment with Swapfiets’ service
  • Chinese students at KIT represented a high-potential niche with 90.91% interest rate, driven by an existing bike-sharing culture
  • Winter tires were the most requested addition across all milieus, with 33 total responses
  • Marketing strategy proposed poster campaigns and guerrilla marketing including mural art and a cycling parade

Reflection

This was my first experience working with a company on a live business problem, through the university course. Leading a team of five through research, survey design, and strategy taught me how to combine different perspectives into one clear deliverable. Conducting street surveys in Karlsruhe gave me hands-on experience with primary data collection and its challenges. Our findings eventually led to the execution of a similar strategy by the company.

## Key Findings

- **Escapist milieu** was selected as the primary target group due to the greatest market potential (~29,300 people) and strongest alignment with Swapfiets' service
- **Chinese students at KIT** represented a high-potential niche with 90.91% interest rate, driven by an existing bike-sharing culture
- **Winter tires** were the most requested addition across all milieus, with 33 total responses
- Marketing strategy proposed poster campaigns and guerrilla marketing including mural art and a cycling parade
